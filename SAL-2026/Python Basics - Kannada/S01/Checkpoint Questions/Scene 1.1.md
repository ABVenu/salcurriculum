#### **Scene 1.1**

1. **Why is a programming language needed to build a digital hotel billing system?**  
   a) To make handwritten bills look more colourful  
   b) To automate repetitive tasks like billing, saving records, and calculating totals quickly  
   c) To replace the need for any staff at the hotel  
   d) To teach customers how to cook food faster  

   **Answer:** b) To automate repetitive tasks like billing, saving records, and calculating totals quickly  

   **Explanation:** Programming turns slow manual work into fast, accurate software that handles billing and tracking without mistakes.

2. **In the Input → Process → Output (IPO) model, which step shows the final result to the user?**  
   a) Input  
   b) Process  
   c) Output  
   d) Storage  

   **Answer:** c) Output  

   **Explanation:** Output is the last step — the program displays or saves the result, such as printing a bus fare of ₹24.

3. **A shop sells 3 notebooks at ₹45 each. Which is the correct Process step before writing code?**  
   a) Display "Total: ₹135"  
   b) Multiply 3 × 45 to get the total cost  
   c) Ask the customer their name  
   d) Install Python on the computer  

   **Answer:** b) Multiply 3 × 45 to get the total cost  

   **Explanation:** Breaking the problem into steps means identifying the calculation (Process) after you know the inputs — quantity and price.
